Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

Jiu Jitsu is the process of grabbing, holding, and manipulating someone's body, with the end goal of immobilizing them by breaking a limb or strangulation

   Take Downs

      You can grab your opponent's head, body, an arm, a leg, or both legs to take them down.

   Positions

      You can hold cross-body, knee on belly, mount, or the back position

   Submissions

      You can attack the shoulder, the elbow, the neck, etc.
